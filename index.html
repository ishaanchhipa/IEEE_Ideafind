# We're back with IdeadFind+

Taking the success of IDEAFIND one step further, IEEE-RAS brings you IDEAFIND+. More knowledge, more mentors, more Networking, more prizes and definitely a lot more fun. IDEAFIND+ is a complete package of what a budding tech enthusiast looks for in a Hackathon. Suitable for all tech and business minds, IDEAFIND+ covers a total of 6 domains, providing a stage for participants to experiment with unusual and out-of-the-box Ideas in front of an experienced and industry-leading panel of judges.

# Tracks

Healthcare

The onset of COVID upon our world just made sure of how crucial healthcare is to all of us, it also showcased a lot of troubles and loopholes faced by the healthcare society.

Education

Pandemic era has completely sabotaged the educational system as a whole by sending whole of 2 years worth of academics into virtual mode which in reality wasn't that as efficient physical classes.

Space Robotics

Space robotics has been an ever demanding field for constant improvement and innovation. There are various problems faced as well for instance deployment,harsh environments, carriers and many as such.

Energy

The need for moving towards a cleaner energy has been a never ending crusade, keeping climate changes in mind we're running against time to adapt to clean energy before it's too late.

Security

Ever since evolution of human beings into social beings, security has become a major part of our lives. Not just us but even security has been constantly evolving with us as we're becoming more and more civilized.

Agriculture

It's a technical contest which is firmly based on the agricultural issues faced widely by farmers and farming sector.

# FAQ

An ideathon event being conducted on 12th to 14th July,2022. IDEAFIND+ provides all the participants a platform to hone their skills and let their acumen shine through Participants will openly ideate and come up with solutions to practical problems to be solved in real time.

IEEE-RAS offers you six tracks to show your aptitude:
1) Healthcare
2) Education
3) Space Robotics
4) Energy
5) Security
6) Agriculture
Keeping these multitude of tracks in mind, each discipline provides the participants an opportunity to come up with creative projects at their end.

Attractive schawgs for all participants, and a prize pool worth more than Rs.30,000 for all the winners!! So choose your track and let your minds fly!

After analysing the first-round submission the teams will get a chance to pitch their idea and get feedback from the panel

The panel is a set of mentors who'll be analysing your presentation and your pitch and give you suggestions for improvement for the final Round

A set of Industry Experts, Tech Enthusiasts and Professors would be evaluating the final round
